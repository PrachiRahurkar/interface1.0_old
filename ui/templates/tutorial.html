1. BERT is a state-of-the-art language model released by Google in 2018. It can be used for a variety of natural language tasks. It is trained on billion words from the Wikipedia corpora.

2. Here, we use BERT for the task of Question-Answering. Given a paragraph and a question, BERT answers the question based on the para.

3. Your job is to provide a paragraph and a question (along with the actual answer) to the trained model. And then hit predict.

4. Modify the paragraph only such that the correct answer is still in the paragraph.

5. You can find some examples of adversaries which are successfully able to fool the model in the tab above.

Enjoy!
